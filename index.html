About IES

Inspire Engineering Services (IES) is a trusted name in industrial infrastructure, fabrication, and engineering excellence since 2018. With a focus on precision, safety, and timely execution, we empower industries to build and maintain critical systems that drive progress.

What We Do

From heavy fabrication and precision installation to civil and boiler maintenance works, IES delivers end-to-end engineering solutions tailored to industrial demands.

Our Expertise

Backed by 17+ years of domain experience, our team blends deep technical knowledge with hands-on execution across sectors like power, manufacturing, and heavy industries.

Why Choose Us

IES stands for safety, trust, and commitment. With zero safety incidents and 100% client satisfaction, we are proud partners in India’s industrial growth journey.

Whether you're planning a new facility or maintaining an existing one, IES offers the tools, team, and technology to bring your industrial vision to life. We don’t just build structures — we build trust, one project at a time.
